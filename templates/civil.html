Design Challenge


Participants are given a plot with dimensions. They need to plan a 1BHK according to Vaastu after that, they need to analyse that plan using STAAD PRO. Judging would be done on the basis of the plan considering vaastu parameters and STAAD PRO analysis for the combination of loads like Earthquake and Wind loads.

Catastrophic Cognition


The participants will be randomly given any civil disaster that occurred in recent years. Participants need to analyse the various parameters that led to disaster and need to present it in front of judges. Participants can present their analysis about disaster by using board or by using power point presentation (not mandatory).
