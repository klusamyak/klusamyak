Navigate around campus using Augmented Reality



    How to Download kluAR:

  1. Go to Playstore and download the Wikitude App

  2. Turn ON the Location(GPS) and MOBILE DATA (or) WIFI

  3. When the app loads Search for kluAR in the search bar.

  4. An icon of KLU should appear with a description of "An Augmented Reality App for KLUniversity"


  5. Click on it and after it loads, point it towards any direction while inside KLUniversity.


  6. Happy Navigating! You should be able to navigate around the campus without a hassle using our Augmented Reality world.
