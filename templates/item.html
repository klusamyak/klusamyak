Koneru Lakshmiah University



 Where every seed is watered and reaped, the tree well-nourished and protected, one can find true greenery in the K L University an impeccable example where the students are well-tutored and invigorated into proficient engineers and managers.

 Standing tall with high-raised buildings amidst the green settings of a picturesque landscape, is the hub of true knowledge blended with culture. This has been a convergence of different arts, skills and talents of students and acts as a melting pot of such flairs and bring best out of them.

  The campus has been aptly named "Green Fields" and the splendid avenue of trees and gardens and it is considered as a divine temple where knowledge is worshipped and every student is treated with same care and nurtured to accomplish their goals.

  This centre of education saw many students come out in life with flying colours and some of them in unique fields of art and sport.

 The support and encouragement provided, nourished small ideas and created young entrepreneurs providing the environment for budding aspirants.
